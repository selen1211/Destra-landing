Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Destra Network (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to respecting the privacy and anonymity of our users (“you,” “your,” or “user”). This Privacy Policy explains how we operate and protect the privacy of all users when interacting with our platform, which includes the Destra GPU Network, AI/LLM Training Platform, OCAI Platform, and other services.

By using our platform, you agree to the principles outlined in this policy.

2. No Collection of Personal Data

Destra Network respects your privacy and does not collect or store any personally identifiable information (PII) or other personal data. We are designed to operate with user anonymity in mind, ensuring that your interactions with the platform are free from data collection or tracking.

Destra Network operates on a global scale and complies with applicable privacy regulations, including but not limited to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for EU users, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) for Californian users, and other relevant international privacy laws.

3. User Responsibilities for Local Laws

Users are responsible for ensuring that their use of Destra Network complies with all local laws and regulations, including restrictions on blockchain technology and decentralized services.

4. Use of Blockchain Data

While Destra Network interacts with decentralized blockchain technology, any data generated from these interactions, such as wallet addresses and transaction history, remains on-chain and is not stored or processed by us. As a decentralized platform, we do not have access to any private keys or other sensitive wallet credentials.

5. WalletConnect and Cookies

Destra Network does not directly use cookies or other tracking technologies to collect data from users. However, the WalletConnect plugin integrated with our platform may utilize cookies or similar technologies to facilitate its services and provide functionality for connecting your wallet to our platform.

WalletConnect is a third-party service that enables secure connections between users’ wallets and decentralized applications (dApps) like Destra Network. While Destra Network does not track or collect personal data, WalletConnect may employ cookies or similar mechanisms to manage and authenticate wallet connections.

We recommend reviewing WalletConnect’s privacy policy for detailed information about their use of cookies and tracking technologies. By using WalletConnect to interact with Destra Network, you agree to WalletConnect’s terms and policies.

6. No Data Sharing

Since Destra Network does not collect any personal information, we do not share user data with third parties, service providers, or other entities.

7. Security

While we do not collect or store personal information, we take necessary precautions to ensure the security of our platform. We use industry-standard security protocols to protect against unauthorized access and ensure that all interactions with our platform remain secure.

8. Blockchain Immutability

It is important to note that while we do not collect personal data, blockchain transactions are public and immutable. Any on-chain activity is visible on the respective blockchain and cannot be altered by Destra Network.

9. Third-Party Links

Our platform may contain links to third-party websites or services that are not operated by Destra Network. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of these external services, and we encourage you to review their privacy policies before interacting with them.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our platform. Any updates will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We recommend reviewing this policy periodically to stay informed of any changes.
